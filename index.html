Our Story

Rooted in Maharashtra's
farming heritage

Drivanta Agro Foods was founded with a single belief: that the richness of Maharashtra's farmlands deserves to reach every kitchen — preserved as nature intended.

Based in Nandurbar, we work directly with local farmers to source the finest seasonal produce. Our sun-drying and dehydration processes use no artificial preservatives, no additives — just controlled temperature, clean air, and patience. The result is produce that retains its natural colour, aroma, and nutritional integrity.
